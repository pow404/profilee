Rambo's Profile

John Rambo

John Rambo is a former U.S. Army Special Forces soldier who is skilled in many aspects of survival, weaponry, hand-to-hand combat and guerrilla warfare. Known for his role in the Vietnam War and beyond, Rambo is a symbol of struggle, courage, and resilience. His stories are captured in various movies, showcasing his journey and battles both abroad and at home.
